Shirts

Guidelines

Design

  • Background color should be dark/muted, neither light nor bright
  • Shirts should be short sleeve, not 3/4 or long sleeve
    Hoodies are acceptable and should have long sleeves
  • Busy designs and art for art's sake should be avoided

Content

  • Logos should be unaltered, not parodies
  • Jokes, sentences, and other text (excluding names and logos) should be avoided
  • Any content must be safe and presentable for work
  • Avoid patriotic and religious content
    Note that patriotism is not limited to the United States
  • Popular topics include:
    • Space agencies (e.g. NASA, Pockocmoc, JAXA, ESA)
    • Engineering groups (e.g. Aerojet Rocketdyne, Boeing, Northrop Grumman, Lockheed Martin, Boston Dynamics, IBM, JPL, DEC)
    • Prestigious research institutions (e.g. MIT, Caltech, ETH Zürich)
